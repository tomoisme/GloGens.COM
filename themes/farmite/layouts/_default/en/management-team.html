Steve Budiono, Chief Executive Officer

Steve Budiono is both President and Chief Executive Officer of the company. He studied at the University of Southern California in Business Administration and International Relations. He later earned Business Leadership Management from the Wharton School of Business at the University of Pennsylvania. With over 15 years of experience in the finance industry, Budiono is one of the Co-founders of the Global Green Energy Solutions Corporation.
